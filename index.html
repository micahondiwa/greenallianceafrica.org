Jackson Ngeno

Chairperson & Co-Founding Partner

The Green Alliance Africa is a youth-led organization that sought issues relating to the environment and climate change. Environmental degradation has been a menace since the beginning of the 21st Century due to establishment of many industries and technology. The Green Alliance Africa will be a useful organization in coming up with modern day environmental solutions for environmental conservation.

The organization will work collaboratively with stakeholders/partners to ensure policies are in place to protect the environment, advocate for policy understanding & implementation/compliance, encourage volunteers and active participation of young people in environmental protection, create a competition platform for young people to showcase their environmental conservation initiatives and conduct wide range of awareness campaigns to communities (online/physical gatherings - colleges, universities, youths and women organizations), produce friendly environmental education material/handbooks to be used in schools, increase sustainable development and participate actively in tree planting activities.

Expected Results

policies

All people to stay within the law. All people informed about their environmental roles, responsibilities and rights. Improved monitoring of environmental impacts.

Young People participation

All youths have changed their behavior and attitude towards environment. 90% of young people are actively participating in protecting and improving the environment.

Awareness

95% of general population understand the environment. 95% of the target population know of the impacts of human behaviors on environment. 95% of the target population are aware of importance of environmental protection. All adults and youths have skills and knowhow to protect environment.

Education Materials and Handbooks

90% of young school going population have been guided and understand climate change and ways to mitigate effects of climate change.

Sustainable Development

All human population get adequate supply of food and wate.

Eco-Tourism Site

One eco-tourism site per region in 5 regions (Lake region, Rift Valley region, Nairobi region, Eastern region and Coastal region). Expect 20million learning/touring visits. 500 jobs created.

Tree Planting

200 Million trees planted and monitored to achieve maturity 1500 job created.
